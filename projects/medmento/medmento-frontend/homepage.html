What we can do for you

Physicians provide specific times to take medications, and abiding by these schedules is essential for the medicine to work properly and to avoid adverse side effects. Unfortunately our loved ones often have trouble remembering to take their medicine, but not to worry. Medmento enables you to send customized, automated messages to the people you love, reminding them to take their medications at their scheduled times.

Step 1

To get up-and-running, all you have to do is create a profile.

Step 2

Pick a date and time to schedule your phone call reminder.

Step 3

A phone call will be sent to the patient when it's time to take their medication.
